WTF?

In case it is not abundantly clear, no this is not my real portfolio site. This is a response to all of the development job postings out there that require XHTML, Dreamweaver, IIS, Microsoft Access, IE 8 compatibility, and the like. There are even some requiring Flash, but I'm not going there. You know who you are.

For all of your hiring managers and recruiters who last looked at your job postings in 1998: Let's get one thing straight. It is curretnly the year 2017 and, believe it or not, the developers you actually want to hire keep up with the times and want to work with those new-fangled technologies. We are not ignoring the past. We are acknowledging the present (and future).
